FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?

ChatGPT doesn't know your margin rules, material pricing, machine capacity, or customer history. Axon is integrated with your actual systems and trained on precision manufacturing workflows. It produces a quote your estimator can send — not a paragraph they have to translate.

What if our parts are too custom for AI?

That's the most common objection — and the one we hear before every new customer sees it work. Axon reads the full spec, not a summary. For truly novel geometries the estimator still applies judgment, but on a draft that's already 80% complete.

Does it integrate with our ERP?

Yes. We have connectors for JobBOSS, Epicor, Global Shop, Shoptech, and custom ERP via API. QuickBooks is included in all plans. Setup is part of the 2-week onboarding — not your IT team's problem.

Who approves things before they go to customers?

You do — always. Every quote, email, and payment goes through your approval queue before executing. You can approve on your phone in under 30 seconds. Nothing customer-facing happens without a human decision.

What is the 30-day ROI guarantee?

If at day 30 you cannot point to measurable time savings and improved quote throughput, we refund your first month's fee. No paperwork. We've only had to pay it once in our first 12 months.

How long does implementation take?

Two weeks to your first automated quote. Week one is integration and configuration. Week two is test runs with your estimators. Your team needs no training — the approval queue is a single screen.
