FAQ

Common questions

How is Axon different from JobBOSS2, ProShop, or E2 Shop?

Legacy ERPs like JobBOSS2, ProShop, and E2 cost $150–$500 per user per month and take months to implement. Most shops use less than 30% of the features. Axon is modular — you pick the single problem costing you the most, and that tool is purpose-built for exactly it. You're running on day one, not month six. No per-user fees — one flat rate, unlimited users.

What is the best software for a CNC machine shop?

The best software for a CNC machine shop depends on your size and biggest pain point. For shops under 50 employees, Axon AI's modular approach works better than full ERPs — you start with the one problem costing you the most (usually scheduling or job costing) at $99/mo with unlimited users, instead of paying $150–500 per user for a system you'll only partially use.

Do my operators need training to use this?

Less than 10 minutes per module. Operators interact with a single screen on a tablet — scan a QR code, tap start, tap complete. Every action is designed to take under 5 seconds or operators won't do it consistently. Manager dashboards are equally direct.

Does it work offline? Our shop floor Wi-Fi is unreliable.

Yes. All operator-facing modules work offline and sync when connection restores. Shop floor tablets can lose internet for hours without losing any data. This was a specific design requirement — manufacturing environments are not office environments.

Will it connect to QuickBooks or our existing software?

QuickBooks integration is included in all plans. ERP connectors (JobBOSS2, E2, Epicor, ProShop) are available on the Enterprise plan. Most small shops do not need deep integration — modules work standalone and data export is compatible with any accounting system.

What is the 30-day ROI guarantee?

Every module has a measurable outcome we agree on before you start — time saved, margin recovered, downtime prevented. If you cannot point to clear ROI at day 30, we refund month one. No paperwork, no negotiation. We've only paid it once in our first year.
