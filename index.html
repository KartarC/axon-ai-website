Common questions

How is Billet different from JobBOSS2, ProShop, or E2?

Legacy ERPs price per user or per employee and typically need a guided implementation measured in weeks or months. Billet is flat-rate, self-serve, and modular — you pick one tool, sign up yourself, and you're running the same day. We publish an honest comparison, including where ProShop is the better choice.

Do my operators need accounts?

No. Every job gets a QR code. Operators scan it on any phone to see the job, work the traveler, and update status — no logins to manage, nothing to train.

What does the AI actually do?

Two things, both grounded in your own data: it suggests quote prices from your shop's comparable past jobs (cited by job number), and it flags any job the moment actual costs approach or cross the quoted price. No generic chatbot.

We run lasers and press brakes, not just mills. Does Billet fit?

Yes — fabrication is a first-class citizen. Machine setup includes lasers, waterjets, plasma, press brakes, and weld stations with their own hourly rates, and the board and costing work identically for cutting and forming work.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

Your data stays put. Pick a plan to continue — or don't, and the account simply pauses. No card is taken up front, so nothing charges automatically.
