FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from just using ChatGPT?
ChatGPT doesn't know your margin rules, your material pricing, your machine capacity, or your customer history. Axon is trained on precision manufacturing workflows and connected to your actual systems. It produces a quote your estimator can send — not a paragraph they have to translate.
What if our parts are too custom for AI?
That's the most common objection — and it's the one we hear from every new customer before they see it work. Axon handles complexity because it reads the full spec, not a summary. For truly novel geometries, the estimator still applies judgment — but on a draft that's already 80% complete.
Does it integrate with our ERP?
Yes. We have connectors for JobBOSS, Epicor, Global Shop, Shoptech, and custom ERP via API. QuickBooks is included in all plans. Setup is part of the 2-week onboarding — not your IT team's problem.
Who approves things before they go to customers?
You do — always. Every quote, every customer email, every payment goes through your approval queue before executing. You can approve on your phone in 30 seconds. Nothing customer-facing happens without a human decision.
What's the 30-day ROI guarantee?
If at day 30 you can't point to measurable time savings and an improvement in quote throughput, we refund your first month's fee. No paperwork, no negotiation. We've only had to pay it once in our first 12 months.
How long does implementation take?
Two weeks to first automated quote. Week one is integration and configuration. Week two is test runs with your estimators. By day 14 you're live. Your team doesn't need training — the approval queue is a single screen.
