Pricing FAQ

Pricing questions, answered

Can I really add unlimited users for one flat price?

Yes, absolutely. Every plan — Starter, Growth, and Shop Suite — includes unlimited users and operators at no extra cost. Your CNC operator, your shipping clerk, your quality manager, and your shop owner can all use it simultaneously. We don't charge by seat because we think it's wrong to penalize shops for having more people using good software.

On the Growth plan — which 3 modules should I pick?

The most common combinations: (1) Production Board + Job Costing + Shop Traveler — for shops whose main pain is scheduling chaos, paper, and unknown margins. (2) Shop Traveler + CoC Generator + Customer Portal — for quality-focused shops doing aerospace or defense work. (3) Job Costing + Shop CRM + Outside Services — for shops focused on profitability and customer retention. We'll help you pick the right 3 on your demo call based on your shop's specific problems.

What is the 30-day ROI guarantee?

Before you start, we agree on a measurable outcome — hours saved per day, margin gap identified, downtime events prevented. At 30 days, we look at it together. If you can't point to clear, real ROI, we refund month one. No negotiation, no paperwork. We've paid it once. Every other shop has been able to point to ROI — usually within the first week.

Can I upgrade from Starter to Growth or Suite later?

Yes. Most shops start with Starter, prove the ROI on one module in the first month, then upgrade. Upgrading is instant — no new implementation, no downtime. Your data from the first module is already in the system and connects to the new modules immediately. You're only billed the difference for the remainder of your billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee or implementation cost?

No setup fee. No implementation cost. No consultants. We configure your shop — machines, op codes, customers, materials — in the same call where we show you the demo. Most shops are fully running on day one. This is specifically designed to be the opposite of a 6-month ERP implementation.

Is annual billing available?

Yes. Annual billing saves you 2 months — effectively getting 12 months for the price of 10. Most shops switch to annual once they've confirmed ROI in the first month or two. Monthly billing is available with no penalty to cancel at any time.
