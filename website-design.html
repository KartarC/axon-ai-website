FAQ

Website design questions

Do I need to provide the content and photos?

We write all the copy based on our discovery call — you just need to review and approve it. Photos help a lot, so we'll give you a simple list of shots to take on your phone (shop floor, machines, finished parts). If you can't get photos, we have access to professional manufacturing stock images.

How long until I start ranking on Google?

New websites typically take 3–6 months to rank for competitive terms. However, local searches ("CNC machining [city]") often rank within 4–8 weeks, especially if your Google Business Profile is optimized. We'll give you realistic expectations based on your market at the discovery call.

What is AEO and why does it matter for a machine shop?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) makes your shop findable when buyers ask AI tools like ChatGPT or Perplexity "find me a precision machine shop in Ontario". More and more procurement teams are using AI assistants to research suppliers. AEO ensures your shop appears in those results by structuring your content so AI systems can cite it accurately.

Can you redesign my existing website or do you start from scratch?

Either. We can redesign an existing site (keeping your domain authority and redirecting old pages correctly) or build from scratch. Redesigns typically cost the same — we quote based on the scope, not whether it's new or a rebuild.

Do I need an Billet software subscription to get a website?

No. The website design service is completely standalone. You can get a website built and SEO done without using any of the shop floor software. That said, the CRM integration only works if you have an Billet CRM module subscription ($99/mo), so most shops add that to capture inbound leads properly.
