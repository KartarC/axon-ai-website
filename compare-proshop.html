Billet vs ProShop ERP

Both are built for machine shops. They're built for different machine shops. Here's an honest breakdown so you pick the right one — even if it isn't us.

The short version: ProShop is a deep ERP/QMS/MES suite built for regulated aerospace, defense, and medical shops — AS9100 audits, CUI hosting, government contracts. If that's your world, it's a strong product. Billet is for the other shops — small commercial machine & fab shops that need scheduling, costing, and travelers running this week, at a price that doesn't scale with headcount.

Which one is right for you?

Choose ProShop if…

  • You hold government or defense contracts with CUI requirements
  • You're pursuing or maintaining AS9100 / ISO certification
  • You have an implementation budget and months of runway
  • You want one deeply-integrated ERP for everything

Choose Billet if…

  • You're a commercial job shop — no CUI, no audit requirements
  • You want to replace the whiteboard and spreadsheets this week
  • You'd rather pay $99–$349 flat than $500+ scaling with headcount
  • You want to start with one tool (scheduling or costing) and grow
  • You run fab equipment too — lasers, waterjets, press brakes
